About MSM-Xtended

We have moved from Cardinal and starting from zero as "MSM Xtended or MSM-X". The Base is GZOSP and thus Special Thanks to @martinusbe & Team GZR for the same. A ROM Based on AOSP, with features from almost all Custom ROMs available without compromising quality or performance. We have tried our best to blend it with all available options, features and stability of different ROMs in to one for better user experience.
